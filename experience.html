Current Role: Regulated Fund Management

Currently serving as the Head of Finance & Accounts and DIFC Authorized CFO for a regulated investment fund specializing in Real Estate and Infrastructure. I oversee an Asset Under Management (AUM) exceeding AED 400 Million. My core mandate involves end-to-end stewardship of fund accounting, SPV management, and ensuring strict adherence to DFSA regulations.

I lead the validation of Net Asset Value (NAV), manage capital calls and distributions, and oversee complex treasury functions including liquidity planning and banking relationships. A critical aspect of my role is partnering with investment teams to evaluate acquisitions and development budgets while maintaining high-governance standards through internal controls.
